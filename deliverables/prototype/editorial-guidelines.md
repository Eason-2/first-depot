# Editorial Guidelines (MVP)

## Writing objective
Publish concise, evidence-based AI news analysis for builders and decision makers.

## Voice and style
- Clear, practical, neutral.
- Avoid hype and absolute claims.
- Explain impact with concrete scenarios.

## Required article structure
1. Why this matters now
2. What happened (facts only)
3. Impact analysis
4. Risks and trade-offs
5. What to watch next
6. Sources and timestamps

## Citation policy
- Every factual statement must map to at least one source.
- For conflicting sources, mark uncertainty explicitly.
- If source confidence is low, set status to HumanReviewRequired.

## Prohibited patterns
- Fabricated quotes or references.
- Unverified benchmark claims.
- Hidden affiliate language in analysis text.

## QA gates before publish
- Citation coverage: pass
- Similarity/plagiarism threshold: pass
- Style policy checks: pass
- Safety policy checks: pass
